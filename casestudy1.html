Improving the User Experience of Glover Pro with Glover Lite

  • Client

    Glover Industries

  • Role

    UI/UX Designer

  • Duration

    2 Months

Project Overview

Glover is a gift card application that supports a wide range of features that include airtime recharge, electric payment, data and internet subscription, airtime to cash, perfect money, and e-tickets.

People are often skeptical about entering sensitive information on online platforms, such as SSN, NIN, KYC, etc. For customers to use the Glover app, they have to go through a lengthy onboarding process that would also involve KYC.

The KYC is important due to the fact that a wallet ( i.e. a bank account is opened for the user) is assigned to every user. The fact that only 2 features of the app (i.e Sell Perfect Money and Sell Giftcard) required the use of the wallet led to a decrease in customer activation because the customer’s primary goal for using the app is to buy gift cards.

The client was also looking for a rebrand, they wanted an application that was more user-friendly and to be in touch with the younger generation. Complaints were also coming from the customer care department as regards usability.

Strategy and Process

UX Audit

Before embarking on our outcomes and research for Glover Lite, we had to conduct a UX review on the existing app to discover pain points. We carried out several interviews with key stakeholders and ran a cognitive walkthrough of the app. Issues we looked out for were navigation, consistency, content, trustworthiness, forms, feedback, error Handling, and accessibility

Research

Competitor Benchmarking

After the UX Audit, we set out to check how the top competitors handled some of the issues highlighted. We also looked for other features and attributes that were not in the Glover system

From the analysis above, we decide to introduce:

Categorization: To group gift cards based on specific needs and purpose.

Gift Card Packages (Custom Gift Card): To offer various gift cards collective packages based on special occasions like birthdays, holidays, and lifestyles.

Info Tips: To provide tips and guides on how the feature works.

Gift a Friend: To enable one to buy gift cards for someone else.

Customized Gift card To all the users to attach customized messages to cards while gifting to a friend.

Stakeholder Interview

We set up a meeting with the stakeholders to discuss the findings. They also added the onboarding was currently failing and it would be nice to have a lite app that would enable users to access the features that don't require KYC,and they would be supporting other countries.

Requirement Gathering

After the interview, we noted a few requirements that are to be added to the application

Dashboard: There wouldn’t be a need for an onboard process for the lite version. Users can just open the app and make use of our services.

Other Payment Methods: With the absence of KYC, there is a need for other payment processes like Transfer, Card, etc.

ADs: There's a need to let the users know more about the app like the benefits of glover pro, special gift card packages, etc.

App Toggle Feature: An easy-to-access toggle button to switch from lite mode to pro mode.
