Project Overview

This project covers the digitization of the entire credit process (loan application) for corporate and commercial customers of the bank.

The process involves customer validation, facility request, collateral review, and fund disbursement.

Processing Loans for our customers is stressful because relationship managers have to go to customers to get information about their business. After validating the customer's business, records, and loan requests have to be pushed for validation through various departments.

Disbursement takes time because all approving supervisors have to be put through all that has been done in order to approve loans.

Business Objective (What)

The business objective of Gazelle Admin is:

Onboard staff - staff would review customers and their business.

Setup Obligor risk rating - parameters for customers to perform a business evaluation on the client side.

Setup Facility risk rating - parameters for customers to request loans client side.

Setup Collateral - parameters for customers to list out the collaterals they would want to lay down.

Create Document uploads - A document module to allow customers to submit documents.

Validate Customers - Validation module to review and validate customer data.

Review Loans and Disburse funds - Review module to review and approve loans once validation is done.
