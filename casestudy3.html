Project Overview

OneBank is a digital mobile application that helps our customers perform certain activities. These activities range from Transfers to Bill Payments, Card Management, etc. There have been rising complaints about the user experience from OneBank. Apparently, the app was initially built without a UX team.

Assumptions

After going through the app, we realized: Users can't access most of the features on the app because they are hidden. They are either located on the side menu or on the account card. The Bills payment and Transfer journey was very complex and frustrating.

Strategy and Process

Customer Interview

We conducted some interviews with some customers and found out that they weren’t pleased with the UI look and feel. It was termed “dull”. “It felt like you hid the features from us”, One customer claimed. Also, day-to-day features like Transfers and Bills also caused some worry. The end-to-end interaction felt off.

We concluded that we work on the Transfer flow and Bills flow with a new set of visual components
