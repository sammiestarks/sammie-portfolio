Project Overview

Proper accounting is essential to keep any business going. Findings show that most SMEs in Nigeria do not keep proper accounting records simply because of the avoidance of the cost of employing the services of professional accountants.

This concern can be addressed by incorporating an SME service on the OneBank App for small business holders to carry out accounting activities with ease and at no additional cost.
