About me

Akwe Samuel is a senior UX designer with over 5 years of experience. He is passionate about creating a pleasing user experience for the target audience.

Some of his greatest works include:

1. A revamped mobile experience of the OneBank application.

2. An admin experience that enables the review and facilitation of loans

3. A mobile application that offers immigration services to new immigrants in Canada.

He is proficient with a lot of design tools like Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Adobe XD, Procreate, Affinity Designer and so many others. Samuel loves the concept of UX design because it's not just about creating an aesthetically pleasing design, but also about solving user problems and meeting business goals.
